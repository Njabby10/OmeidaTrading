Welcome to Omeida Trading 178

Spreading Love and Care in Our Community

About Us

We are an established non-profit organisation that serves to give love and care to individuals in the community who are in dire need of them. Our organisation started in 2005 with a feeding scheme for the homeless in Hillbrow, Johannesburg and has grown to cater for other less fortunate in the community through the financial assistance of various sectors and entities.

The biggest breakthrough of the organisation came in 2007 when I met Prophet TB Joshua of the Synagogue Church of All Nations, who taught us the importance of giving without any expectation, how to help the less privileged, 'Better is not good enough the best is yet to come.'

Scope of Work

Omeida Trading 178 aims to provide the homeless with food on a daily basis; to assist the underprivileged with food and other immediate needs they need assistance with; and old age homes with food, assistance with facility maintenance amongst other things. The proposed funding of this program will make it possible for our organisation to carry out this mission for the next 12 months.
